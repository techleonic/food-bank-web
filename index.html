Who We Are

Neighbors Helping Neighbors

GrainGood Food Bank is a nonprofit organization dedicated to ending hunger in our community. We collect, store, and distribute essential food items to people who face food insecurity.

Since 2010, we've provided over 5 million meals to families, seniors, and individuals in need. Our mission is to ensure everyone has access to nutritious food.

Our Services

How We Help The Community

Food Distribution

Weekly grocery packages containing grains, beans, fresh produce, dairy, and protein sources for individuals and families.

Nutrition Education

Workshops and resources on preparing healthy meals on a budget, maximizing nutritional value, and reducing food waste.

Home Delivery

Grocery delivery service for seniors, disabled individuals, and those without transportation to access our physical location.
